About Ferrara's Auto Detailing

Over 20 years of professional auto detailing excellence

Meet Nick Ferrara

Founder & Master Detailer

With over 20 years of experience in the automotive detailing industry, Nick Ferrara has built a reputation for excellence and attention to detail that is second to none. What started as a passion for cars has evolved into a thriving business serving the Centerville, OH area and beyond.

Nick's commitment to quality and customer satisfaction has earned him hundreds of five-star reviews and a loyal customer base. He takes pride in treating every vehicle as if it were his own, ensuring that each customer receives the highest level of service.
